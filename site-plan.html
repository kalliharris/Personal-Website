Overview

Purpose

The purpose of this website is to create a fast and simple way to order the perfect set of custom nails to apply easily yourself. This will be a way for the customers to place an order and receieve a package on their doorstep with these nails from me, shipped to anywhere in the world.

Audience

My audience is anyone who wants to have a professional looking manicure from home.

Content

Home page

  • The top of the header will contain a link to "home" to allow the audience to view the entire page from the beginning. It will be up with the other two links to my two child pages on my website.
  • Also in the header, next to the "home" page link, will be a "Designs" link. The "designs" link will take viewers to a full page of most just images. These images will be of my previous nail sets that I have created and completed. This will be an opportunity for customers to have an idea of a possible nail design they may want for themselves. I will put them in rows of 2 or 3 images at a time, and it will be a page they can scroll down through and look at all the images. Possibly in the future, I will divide the images even more into sections like 'holiday','summer', 'fall', etc.
  • Next to both the "home" page link and the "designs" page link, there will be an "order" page link. The "order" link will be linked to another page that will allow viewers to place an order for their own custom set of nails. This page will have a way to create new clients into a system and collect their information such as: name, address, email, phone number, etc. to be able to send them their own set of nails to their doorstep.
